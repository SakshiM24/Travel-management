Bus Rider Rules & Regulations

The rules and regulations listed below are the result of careful study in day to day operation of our Transport system. Please take the time to go over the rules & regulations so that you will know what is expected of you while commuting in the bus.

1. You are to report at designated stops/pickup points at least five (5) minutes prior to scheduled time. The Departure timing (schedule) at each stops/ pickup point will be strictly followed by Bus Driver. You will be responsible in case you do not reach in time at your pick up point.

2. Safety is of prime importance & therefore you are responsible for the safety of yourself while going to or from the bus stop and while waiting for the bus.

3. You are expected to conduct yourself in a proper manner at bus stops. You are not to inflict damage to buses, personal property, or public property.

4. For your own safety wait for the bus on the shoulder of the road, form a line to get on the bus, do not run after a moving bus and do not disembark at traffic signals.

5. Occupy the seats that are vacant and keep the front seats vacant for lady co passengers.

6. Remain seated at all times. Wait until the bus has come to a complete stop before attempting to enter or leave the bus.

7. Sit erect with your feet on the floor. Keep your head, arms and hands inside the bus.

8. Hold belongings in your hand or place them on the provided overhead shelf. Do not leave them in the aisles or near emergency door.

9. You are not allowed to play your radios, cassette tape or cd players without headphones.

10. Avoid unnecessary noise. The driver cannot be distracted while the bus is in motion. Help the driver to assure safety at all times.

11. Do not leave the bus without the driver's consent. No unauthorized stops will be made.

12. Cross at least 10 feet in front of the bus when crossing the road. Do not cross in back of the bus.

13. Be courteous. The use of vulgar or profane language, fighting, pushing and shoving will not be tolerated.

14. Help to keep the bus clean. No eating, No smoking & No Chewing of Tabaco on the bus!

15. Damage to the interior or the exterior of the bus will result in payment for the damages and in suspended bus riding privileges.

16. You are forbidden to carry alcohol, drugs, flammables, ammunition, explosives, firearms, knives, or any other dangerous materials or objects on the bus.

17. "Guests" will not be allowed to board the bus.

18. Produce your Bus Pass on demand to authorized person by Admin.

19. Carry your Bus Pass always with you. Please board at your Designated/ Opted stop only. If found without pass you will be asked to De-Board by Route SPC/ Admin or Bus driver.

20. You will ride only the bus to which you have been assigned. Do not deviate from your Shift & Route.

21. For temporary change in shift get a temporary pass issued (1 day in advance) from Admin with approval from approving authority. Validity 1 day return trip in a requested shift.

22. For change in shift; minimum 2 day prior intimation to be given to Admin with approval of approving authority. Surrender your old pass and get a new bus pass issued from Admin.

23. Report loss of Bus Pass to Admin immediately & collect duplicate pass. You will be charged Rs.30/- for duplicate Pass.

24. It is important to remember that bus transportation is a privilege, not a right

.

25. In case of violation of rules and regulations Management decision will be binding.

I have read the Bus Rider Rules and Regulations of “AEQUS SEZ Pvt Ltd” and I agree to abide by them.
